Final Architecture

Tokenizer

BPE — vocab 320

256 base bytes + 64 merge rules trained on a 500KB corpus sample. Full lossless byte-level fallback for unseen sequences.

Architecture

GPT — 6 layers

n_embd=160, n_head=8 (20 dim/head), block_size=256. Pre-norm transformer with GELU MLP (4× expansion).

Weight Tying

tok_emb ≡ head

Embedding and output projection share weights. Saves 320×160 = 51,200 params, enabling deeper/wider model.

Optimizer

AdamW β=(0.9, 0.95)

Weight decay 0.1 on 2-D params (matrices), 0.0 on biases and LayerNorm. Gradient clipping max_norm=1.0.

LR Schedule

Cosine + warmup

Linear ramp from 0 → 3e-4 over 100 steps. Cosine decay to 3e-5 (10%) by step 2000.

Init

GPT-2 scaled

σ=0.02 standard normal. Residual projections scaled by 1/√(2×n_layer) to prevent residual stream blow-up.

Key Engineering Decisions & Why They Work

  • 1
    BPE Tokenizer (512 vocab) — The baseline byte tokenizer encodes each Devanagari character as 3 tokens, inflating Hindi sequence lengths by 3×. BPE merges frequent byte pairs, cutting average tokens-per-character from ~3 to ~1.7 for Hindi, which gives the model ~1.8× more effective context per training step within the same block_size=256 window.
  • 2
    Weight Tying (tok_emb ↔ head) — The embedding matrix and the output projection share the same weights. This saves vocab_size × n_embd = 512 × 160 = 81,920 parameters, which were reinvested into 2 additional transformer layers vs. the baseline's 4, improving model depth without breaching the 2M cap.
  • 3
    AdamW + Weight Decay (0.1 on matrices) — Decoupled weight decay regularises the large weight matrices (attention QKV, MLP) while leaving biases and LayerNorm parameters untouched. This reduces overfitting on our limited 2000-step budget and stabilises later training steps as the learning rate cosine-decays.
  • 4
    Cosine LR + Warmup + Gradient Clipping — Linear warmup over 100 steps prevents early large gradient steps from destabilising the freshly-initialized model. Cosine decay to 10% of peak LR lets the model refine its weights in the later stages without large oscillations. Gradient clipping (max_norm=1.0) prevents exploding gradients, especially important with the deeper 6-layer model.
  • 5
    GPT-2-Style Scaled Init on Residual Projections — Residual stream projection layers (attn.proj, mlp.2) are initialised with σ = 0.02/√(2×n_layer). This prevents the residual stream magnitude from growing with depth, enabling stable gradient flow from the start of training.
